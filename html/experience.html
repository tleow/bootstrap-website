First Impressions

The feeling I had when I walked through the gates of SP for the very first time was an experience I would probably forget.

No matter how excited I was or nervous I was at that point in time, those feelings had gradually faded the more time I had spent in the school. In these short five months of meeting new friends, gaining knowledge and exploring my interests in SP, I have come to have a better understanding of myself and what lies ahead for me in the future.

Hence, although it may be too early to say so, I will probably never forget the day that I would leave the gates of SP a little under three years from now, as someone who has built upon their values and proficiency to better the community in ways that I can.

My favourite places in SP

FC1 is by far the best FC in SP. Despite being a 15 minutes walk from SoC, my friends and I goes there at least twice a week. There is a myriad of food options available, while also being super affordable. My favourite food there is the Breakfast Set C which comes with a warm cup of coffee, 2 soft-boiled eggs and a large kaya toast!

Again, it is worth the long walk from SoC to chill and study in my favourite spot! My friends and I would book pods to have a private space to study and blast music.

Since the gym is free for SP students, I would take advantage of it and go to the gym at least once every week to exercise and keep myself fit.
